Capstone: OurAccess

Promoting accessibility on campus

Our team is creating a website that maps accessible routes between campus buildings that factors in detours and individual accessibility needs. Our project will also allow students to alert each other to broken accessibility features on campus or blocked paths to give disabled students the most accurate route possible on a given day. Disabled individuals will no longer have to spend limited time and energy searching for accessible routes on campus, removing one of the many barriers in academia.

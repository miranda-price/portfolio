Capstone: OurAccess

Increasing accessibility on campus

Our senior design team is developing a website that maps accessible routes between campus buildings that factors in detours and individual access needs, while also allowing people to report broken elevators and push door buttons to maintenance. Our project focuses on those with mobility issues, while striving to be accessible to all by following WCAG standards.

I serve as the lead programmer, research assistant, and sensitivity advisor on the team.
